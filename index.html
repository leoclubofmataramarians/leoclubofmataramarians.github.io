About Us

Founded in 2023 at St. Mary’s Convent, Matara, the Leo Club of Matara Marians has quickly grown into a dynamic force for youth leadership and community service. Inspired by the guidance of staff advisor Lion Lady Yuagnthi Gunasekara, charter president Leo Irushi Abeygunawardhana, and an impactful orientation by former District President Leo Dilitha Ranjuna FLM, the club embraced the core values of Leoism — service, teamwork, and kindness.

Under the leadership of Leo Hiruni Nawarathne in its second term, the club expanded its impact through notable initiatives such as the “Frosty Fest” Christmas carol program and Matara’s first-ever “Colours of Love” Holi Festival. Now in its third year, operating under Leo District 306 D8 with District President Leo Anushka Liyanage and club president Leo Bisandi Mansari, the Marians Leos continue to strengthen their legacy through meaningful service and sustained community development in Matara.
